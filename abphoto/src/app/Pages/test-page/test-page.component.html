About Me

Starting my photography career during my time at Sammamish High School, I landed a sales position at Kit's Camera. My passion for developing and printing photos soon led me to work in the 1-hour photo booth. It's likely that I've printed some of your photos from your film days in Factoria, Issaquah, South Center, or Klahanie. Throughout the years, I've captured various events, weddings, and commercial products, but I eventually discovered my true calling in photographing people. Feel free to contact me via email, phone or text with any questions you have.

Lenovo Headshots

Turning the Lenovo Redmond office into a dynamic photoshoot set, I recently had the pleasure of capturing the essence of the brilliant minds behind Lenovo's software engineering team. Armed with a cart full of photography equipment, we swiftly transformed their workspace into a professional studio. In just 20 minutes, we were ready to roll. Each team member, with their busy schedules in mind, was scheduled for a brief but personalized 15-minute session. The result? A collection of three diverse poses, ensuring everyone had options to showcase their best professional self. Post-session, individuals had the opportunity to select their favorite shots, which were then meticulously edited and sent to the Lenovo marketing department for their intended use. It was a seamless and efficient process, capturing the individuality and expertise of the team in every frame. #LenovoTeam #ProfessionalPortraits #OnLocationPhotography

About Me

Starting my photography career during my time at Sammamish High School, I landed a sales position at Kit's Camera. My passion for developing and printing photos soon led me to work in the 1-hour photo booth. It's likely that I've printed some of your photos from your film days in Factoria, Issaquah, South Center, or Klahanie. Throughout the years, I've captured various events, weddings, and commercial products, but I eventually discovered my true calling in photographing people. Feel free to contact me via email, phone or text with any questions you have.

Lighting

Good lighting is crucial for a great headshot. The lighting should be even and natural-looking, highlighting the subject's best features.

Expression

The subject's expression should be natural and confident, with a friendly and approachable demeanor.

Composition

A great headshot should be well-composed, with the subject placed in the center of the frame and their head and shoulders in clear view.
